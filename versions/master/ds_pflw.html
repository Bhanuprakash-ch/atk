Process Flow Examples¶

These are some examples to show how to work with the tools and algorithms in ATK. Cumulative Sum is a sequence of partial sums of a given sequence. Naive Bayes is a basic classifier. LDA, short for Latent Dirichlet Allocation, performs topic modeling, which is a form of clustering. Graphs are a mathematical representation of connections and networks.
